Our Mission

We collect excess food from the Cinco Ranch High School cafeteria and distribute it to local shelters, food banks, and those in need in our community.

Collect

We gather untouched, excess food from our school cafeteria that would otherwise go to waste.

Distribute

We partner with local organizations to get this food to those who need it most in our community.

Educate

We raise awareness about food waste and hunger issues, inspiring others to take action.

About Cinco Ranch High School

President

Cinco Ranch High School Initiative

Cinco Ranch High School has always been at the forefront of community service and innovation. Our Campus Cares program is a shining example of how students can make a real difference in their community. What started as a simple idea to reduce food waste has grown into a movement that feeds hundreds while bringing our school together.

The success of this program at Cinco Ranch has been so remarkable that we're now working to expand it to other high schools in the district. Our goal is to create a network of schools working together to reduce food waste and fight hunger across our community.

- Naitik, President of Campus Cares
